
ROI Analysis
Downtime Exposure

Daily transaction value: 500 crore INR

Hourly blocked transaction value:

500 / 24 = 20.83 crore INR per hour

Current uptime: 99.92%

Approximate annual downtime:

0.08% of 8760 hours = 7 hours

Blocked annual payment volume at current uptime:

7 x 20.83 = 145.81 crore INR

Target uptime: 99.99%

Maximum annual downtime:

0.01% of 8760 hours = 52.6 minutes

Financial Justification

If DR reduces downtime from 7 hours to under 1 hour annually, PaySecure reduces blocked transaction exposure by more than 120 crore INR per year.

Non-Financial Benefits
Lower regulatory risk
Better merchant confidence
Better audit posture
Faster incident recovery
Stronger NPCI resilience alignment
Reduced reputational damage
Recommendation

The additional DR investment of approximately 3.2 to 4.8 crore INR per year is justified because the potential downtime exposure is much higher than the added infrastructure cost.
